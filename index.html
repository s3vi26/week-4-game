Crystals Collector!

How To Play:

At the beginning of the game you will be given a random number.
There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your Total Score.
In order to win the game, your Total Score number must match (and not go OVER) the random number.
Keep in mind the values of the crystals are hidden from you, until you click on them.
Also, once you win/lose the game, the game will regenerate new values for the hidden crystals & random number. GOOD LUCK! :)
